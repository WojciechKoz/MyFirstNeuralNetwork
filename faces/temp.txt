<div class="bio col-sm-7 col-md-8 col-lg-6">
<h2>About<a id="btn-edit" href="#" class="btn btn-default btn-sm btn-edit" data-url="xxxtentacion.html" title="Send Suggestion"><i class="icn icn-edit"></i></a></h2>
<p>Collaborator with the music collective <a href="https://www.famousbirthdays.com/bands/members-only.html">Members Only</a> who became known for songs such as "Look At Me," "SAD!" and "Jocelyn Flores." He released studio albums titled 17 and ? in 2017 and 2018. </p>
<h2>Before Fame</h2>
<p>He spent his childhood in youth programs. He posted a first track, called "Vice City," to his SoundCloud account in March of 2014. </p>
<h2>Trivia</h2>
<p>His album 17 charted at number two on the US Billboard 200 as well as in numerous European countries. He created a gaming YouTube channel which reached 5.5 million subscribers.</p>
<h2>Family Life</h2>
<p>His name was Jahseh Dwayne Onfroy. His father was incarcerated for much of his upbringing, leaving him in the hands of his mother. He was a middle child between his older sister Arianna Drew and his younger brother Aiden. He was shot and killed in Southern Florida on June 18, 2018. His son <a href="https://www.famousbirthdays.com/people/gekyume-onfroy.html">Gekyume Onfroy</a> was born seven months after his death.</p>
<h2>Associated With</h2>
<p>He was featured on the <a href="https://www.famousbirthdays.com/people/robb-bank.html">Robb Banks</a> mixtape C2: Death of My Teenage. </p>
